### law-001: Fair Labor Standards Act
Establishes minimum wage, overtime pay, recordkeeping, and youth employment standards for full-time and part-time workers in the private sector and in federal, state, and local governments.

### law-002: Family and Medical Leave Act
Allows eligible employees to take unpaid, job-protected leave for specified family and medical reasons with continuation of group health insurance coverage.

### law-003: Occupational Safety and Health Act
Ensures safe and healthful working conditions by setting and enforcing standards and by providing training, outreach, education, and assistance.

### law-004: Equal Pay Act
Requires that men and women in the same workplace be given equal pay for equal work.

### law-005: Title VII of the Civil Rights Act
Prohibits employment discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, or national origin.

### law-006: Age Discrimination in Employment Act
Protects individuals who are 40 years of age or older from employment discrimination based on age.

### law-007: Americans with Disabilities Act
Prohibits discrimination against individuals with disabilities in all areas of public life, including jobs.

### law-008: National Labor Relations Act
Protects the rights of employees and employers, encourages collective bargaining, and curbs certain private sector labor and management practices.

### law-009: Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act
Protects service members' reemployment rights when returning from a period of service in the uniformed services.

### law-010: Employee Retirement Income Security Act
Sets minimum standards for most voluntarily established pension and health plans in private industry.

### law-011: Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act
Requires employers to provide 60 days’ advance notice of covered plant closings and mass layoffs.

### law-012: Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act
Prohibits discrimination on the basis of genetic information with respect to health insurance and employment.

### law-013: Pregnancy Discrimination Act
Prohibits discrimination on the basis of pregnancy, childbirth, or related medical conditions.

### law-014: COBRA (Consolidated Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act)
Gives workers and their families the right to choose to continue group health benefits provided by their group health plan for limited periods.

### law-015: Fair Credit Reporting Act (Employment Context)
Regulates the collection and use of consumer credit information for employment purposes.
